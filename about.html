Our Story & vision

A "Saaj" is a single silver component - the small building block that, when joined together piece by piece, becomes a complete finished product such as a payal (anklet) or other piece of silver jewelry. Every Shankh, Pankha, Chakri, or Bund Saaj we make is one such piece, supplied in bulk to manufacturers who assemble them into the final ornaments sold across India.

Today, we supply a wide range of Saaj components - from classic Shankh and Pankha designs to custom fancy designs - while keeping the same family values we started with: trust, quality, and long-term relationships.
